How We Work

The Path from
Hide to Excellence

Every Leather Life product travels through a six-stage manufacturing journey — each step governed by strict quality standards and expert craftsmanship.

01
Material Selection

Hand-selecting premium full-grain hides from certified sustainable sources, inspected for grain uniformity.

02
Precision Cutting

Computer-aided and hand cutting ensures zero waste and dimensional accuracy within 0.1mm tolerance.

03
Expert Stitching

Waxed linen and bonded nylon threads, hand-stitched or machine-guided by master craftsmen.

04
Edge Finishing

Burnished, painted, and hand-buffed edges that define the character and longevity of every product.

05
Quality Inspection

Multi-point QC — tensile testing, colour fastness, hardware durability, and cosmetic finish verification.

06
Premium Packaging

Export-grade packaging with branded tissue, dust bags, and moisture control for global delivery.

FAQ

Questions About
Our Process

Everything you need to know before partnering with Leather Life for your manufacturing and export requirements.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?+
Our standard MOQ is 50 units per style for most leather products. For bespoke and custom-designed items, we evaluate requirements on a case-by-case basis. Bulk orders above 500 units receive priority scheduling and preferential pricing.
Do you offer private label and OEM manufacturing?+
Yes. We offer complete private label services including custom branding, embossing, debossing, logo placement, branded hardware, and fully customized packaging. Our OEM capabilities cover product development from sketch to finished sample.
What types of leather do you work with?+
We work with full-grain leather, top-grain leather, genuine leather, nubuck, suede, and vegan-friendly alternatives including PU and recycled leather composites. All materials are sourced from certified tanneries with traceable supply chains.
What are your lead times for production?+
Standard lead time for production orders is 30–45 days from approved sample sign-off. Expedited production of 20–25 days is available for repeat orders or standard catalogue products. Custom development samples take 10–14 working days.
What quality certifications do you hold?+
Leather Life holds ISO 9001:2015 certification and complies with REACH, CPSIA, and Oeko-Tex Standard 100 requirements. We are regularly audited by third-party agencies including SGS and Bureau Veritas.
Do you handle international shipping and export documentation?+
Yes. We manage complete export logistics including freight forwarding, customs documentation, certificates of origin, packing lists, commercial invoices, and compliance paperwork. We work with DHL, FedEx, and freight carriers for global delivery.
